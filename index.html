Welcome to Mr Price

serving fashion since 1985

Mr Price Group is a popular retail company from South Africa that sells affordable clothing, homeware, and sportswear. Founded in 1985, it is known for offering trendy products at low prices, making fashion accessible to a wide range of customers. Over the years, Mr Price has grown into one of the country's leading retail brands with stores across Africa and an online presence.
