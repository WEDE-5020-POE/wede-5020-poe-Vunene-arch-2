Mr Price started in 1985 as a small clothing store with a simple idea, make fashionable clothes affordable for everyone. Founded by Laurie Chiappini and Stewart Cohen, the brand quickly grew because people liked its low prices and trendy styles. Over time, Mr Price Group expanded beyond clothing into homeware, sportswear, and financial services, opening stores across South Africa and later online. Today, it's one of the country's biggest retail brands, still focused on its original mission: offering good-looking products at affordable prices.

Mission Statement

To add value to our customers' lives and worth to our partners' lives, while caring for the communities and environments in which we operate.

Vision Statement

To become a top performing international retailer.

Mr Price Group Management Team
