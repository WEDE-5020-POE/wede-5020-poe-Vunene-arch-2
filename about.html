Mr Price Group is a leading cash-based, omni-channel retailer in South Africa, focused on delivering fashion-value to customers. Established in 1985, the group operates in various segments, including apparel, homeware, sportswear, financial services, and telecoms. With a presence in South Africa and several African countries, Mr Price Group aims to provide superior value and a wide range of products to its customers. The company is committed to sustainability and has made significant strides in reducing single-use plastic and promoting education through the Mr Price Foundation.

Mission Statement

To add value to our customers' lives and worth to our partners' lives, while caring for the communities and environments in which we operate.

Vision Statement

To become a top performing international retailer.

Mr Price Group Management Team

The Mr Price Group management team is led by key executives such as Ayanda Mnculwane, Chief Executive Officer, Liziwe Masoga, Chief People Officer, and Nondumiso Zungu, Group Legal Manager. The management team is responsible for overseeing the group's operations, ensuring compliance, and leading the company's strategic direction. Mr Price Group
