## Project Overview

Mr Price started as a small clothing store in 1985 with simple idea-sell fashionable clothes at affordable prices. Over time it grew into a major retail brand by expanding its product range and opening stores across South Africa and beyond. Today it is recognised for good value and keeping up fashion trends while affordable.

Vision Statement
To be the most loved retailer in Africa.

Mission Statement
To deliver exceptional value and experiences that improve people’s lives.

Target Audience
The Mr Price Group targets price-conscious, trend-aware consumers, primarily young adults (18–35) and families in middle- to lower-income segments.
Its audience consists of individuals who value affordable, fashionable products, including students, entry-level professionals, and households seeking value for money. The brand also appeals to digitally active shoppers who prefer convenient online purchasing and respond to promotions and discounts.
Overall, Mr Price focuses on a value-driven market segment that prioritises both affordability and style.




## Website Goals and Objectives

The main goal of the Mr Price website is to sell products online while giving customers an easy and enjoyable shopping experience. It also helps the business grow by promoting the brand and keeping customers engaged.
To measure success, the company focuses on clear KPIs such as online sales, how many visitors actually buy (conversion rate), how much customers spend per order, and how often they return. It also looks at website performance, like how fast pages load and how many users leave without exploring, as well as operational factors like how quickly orders are delivered.
By tracking these specific indicators, Mr Price can see what’s working, improve weak areas, and stay competitive while continuing to offer affordable fashion.
